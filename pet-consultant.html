BEHAVIOUR CONSULTATION

My interest in dog training has been moving me to learn everything I could find in this field since I was a kid.
I am a proud owner of a very sensitive dog and we went together through relocation to a different country, which required additional attention to the dog's mental, emotional and physical comfort and health.
I work with shy, insecure and fearful dogs. During our online consultation, we will be working out training schemes, incorporating them in your daily life and talk a lot about what dogs need, how they feel and communicate in the modern world.

Together we will learn the unique traits and behaviour patterns your dog has and will find a way to shape them.
